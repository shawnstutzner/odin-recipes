Lasagna

Lasagna is a meal that originated in Italy in the city of Naples during the Middle Ages.

It is a dish with layers of pasta that can have different fillings such as vegetables, mince, or other options of your choice. It has cheese melted over the top.

It is a great family meal that can be enjoyed all year round.

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Spices
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Beef and/or sausage
  • Egg
  • Mozzarella
  • Onion
  • Tomoto Paste
  • Parsely
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
